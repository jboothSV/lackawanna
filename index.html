Welcome To Lackawanna County

Whether you’re looking to find a relaxing escape from it all or check off some items on your bucket list, Lackawanna County offers something for everyone. Know for our historic sites, sports, outdoor adventures, and art & culture—with so much to do, plan to spend the weekend and see how rewarding life can be where City Meets Country. Lackwanna County is truly the best of both worlds!
