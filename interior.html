Where You Wanna Winter

When our blue sky turns to white and the first snowflake touches the tip of your nose, you know the winter season has hit Northeastern Pennsylvania. Cold hands throw that first snowball and warm hearts cozy up with loved ones as the anticipation builds for all that our winter season offers. Montage Mountain is packed with powder. Grab your friends, grab your board, and shred! Or strap on your skis and tackle the downhills. Just minutes from downtown Scranton, Montage offers everything from bunny hills to expert courses, so bring the whole family. When you’re ready for a break, kick your feet up in the lodge, enjoy a cup of cocoa or a craft brew and warm-up beside the fireplace.

wanna get cozy?

Montage Mountain Ski Resort and Waterpark is conveniently located in Northeast Pennsylvania’s scenic Pocono Mountain Region. The 400 acre resort doubles as a skier’s paradise by winter and warm-weather fun zone by summer. The winter features 27 fully groomed ski slopes, the longest snow tubing lanes in PA, 2 terrain parks, 7 lifts, and is 1 of 7 ski resorts in the country, and the only in PA, to offer Airboarding! With ski terrain ranging in difficulty from beginner to double black diamond, including the 2nd steepest slope on the east coast, “White Lightning,” Montage Mountain is a must-visit in NEPA. Montage Mountain's summer features include a giant wave pool, Lazy River, and ZipRider, and is the perfect destination for excitement-seekers.

Elk Mountain Ski Resort

Elk Mountain Ski Resort is a 'hidden gem' in the Endless Mountains on the summit of North Knob near Union Dale, PA. It is located just 30 miles north of Scranton. The mountain receives an average of five feet of natural snow per season, but snowmaking covers 100-percent (180 acres) of the terrain. Elk Mountain boasts approx 1,000 feet of vertical excitement, and their 7 lifts (5 doubles, 1 quad, and 1 cable tow in the terrain park) give access to 27 slopes that include 2 terrain parks and 11 black diamond trails for skiers and snowboarders--offering some of the most challenging skiing in the region. Warm up or recharge at the day lodge, restaurant, and snack bar! Learn to ski at Elk's ski school. Stop into their ski and snowboard shop for some gear, which also offers equipment rentals.
