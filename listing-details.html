Welcome to Hotel Anthracite, a tribute to the heritage of Carbondale. Relax in their modern hotel with touches of history all around you. The ambiance of the hotel will transport with a peek into the past of Carbondale with all the comforts of modern amenities. They are a full-service lodging, dining, and event local featuring kōl Steakhouse restaurant--open 7 days a week for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Plan your next event at Gravity Hall onsite, a 500 person ballroom great for meetings and weddings. Ski Packages available with Elk Mountain.
